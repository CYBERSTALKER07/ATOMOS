# Abstracts

**Language:** en

**Label:** English

**Title:** Pegasus: Synchronized Fabric Of Commerce For Multi-Role Logistics

**Abstract:** An integrated logistics ecosystem reduces operator friction by preserving commercial intent across supplier, retailer, driver, and payload roles through a shared orchestration fabric. A single retail gesture can atomically mature into supplier-scoped orders, authoritative state, settlement finality, route activation, live telemetry, replay-safe proof, and future machine execution, allowing human convenience and machine discipline to coexist within one synchronized state architecture.

**Language:** es

**Title:** Pegasus: Ecosistema Logistico Multirrol Orquestado por Maquina

**Abstract:** Un ecosistema logistico coordina los roles de proveedor, minorista, conductor y operador de payload mediante una capa compartida de control que abarca interfaces, pedidos transaccionales, recuperacion de pagos, optimizacion de rutas, telemetria en vivo, prediccion de demanda con IA, sincronizacion de pruebas fuera de linea y logistica inversa.
